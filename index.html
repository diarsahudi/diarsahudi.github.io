About Diar Sahudi

Diar Sahudi is an Indonesian singer based in Yogyakarta. He began writing songs in the early 90s and has released several albums and singles with various bands. In 2018, he released his solo album titled "Rediscovered" recorded live, showcasing his musical journey. His music blends pop rock and ballad, reflecting his evolution as an artist over the decades.
